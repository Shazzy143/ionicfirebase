About Us

Vision:

To be a ‘Centre of Excellence’ in school examination and assessment informed by research and innovation and integrated with technology for raising education standard in order to enable young generation to compete nationally and globally.

Mission:

To conduct fair, transparent and authentic Secondary School Certificate (SSC), Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) and other examinations for assessing student learning outcomes which are valued by higher education institutions nationally and globally.
